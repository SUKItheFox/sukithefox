About Suki

Programming is much like witchcraft, have to give the right command in order for the spell to work properly. Although I do not know the full book, I work every day on understanding new spells and obtaining deeper and wider knowledge of languages.

While living in a log house between the forests and swamps, I've acquired a magic root wand from the fairy mother of the swamp, that makes my code clean and the apps developed functional. This trade offers me the delight to live life towards my full potential, by creating new software and communication tools, connecting life everywhere. My particularly rehearsed witchcraft lies in HTML, HAML, CSS, SASS, Ruby on Rails, Javascript, jQuery and MySQL.. Currently also obtaining new spells of Python.
