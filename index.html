About Suki

I am seeking to leverage solid development skills with a focus on collaboration, communication, passion, and creativity as a programmer aiming to spread environmental awareness and help to solve global issues through the enhancement of technology.

What I love about programming is that you know when it is right or wrong, there is no space for ambiguity.
My programming style is well structured and commented, making it easy to read and work on by colleagues.
In the 'projects' section of this website, you'll find some examples of the projects I have worked on previously:
* Groen Geluk is a study module application made mainly in Ruby on Rails that has a welcome page, forum area, 'currently online users' feature, private messaging application, user authentication and file uploads possibility.
* Octo Marketing Website is a one-pager for a marketing company.
* Notey is a Rails app, that makes writing notes down online easy for every user.
* Board of Pins is a Rails app that imitates Pinterest.
* Vakantieparels is a one-pager for a travel agency.
* To-Bee is an artists' platform that is written mainly in Ruby. It is a social platform with a forum, messager, personal blog feature and an online shop.
* Crypto Portfolio is written in Ruby on Rails and helps people to keep track of their cryptocurrency amounts, current market value and different personal wallets.
* To Do list is a simple Web/Android/IOS app made with Ruby.
* Smithy's Store is an example of a responsive Web design for a shop.
* Cablechat is a primitive chat application written with Rails and ActionCable.
